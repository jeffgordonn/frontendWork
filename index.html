Weight Lifting: Core Three Lifts

Bench Press
The bench press works the chest, triceps, and shoulders.
Squats
Squats can work various muscles in the legs. The width of the stance will determine to what degree what muscles take over, but the primary muscles are the quads, glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.
Conventional Deadlifts
Conventional deadlifts work essentially every muscle in your body. Their primary benefactors will be your posterior chain, hamstring and lower back.
